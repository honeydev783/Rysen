RYSEN Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2025

RYSEN is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure, transparent experience while using our Spiritual Companion Chat Progressive Web App (PWA). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in compliance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). RYSEN is not an EU entity but may be used globally, and we implement measures to ensure compliance for all users. By signing up for RYSEN, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

1. Age Restriction

RYSEN is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 16. If you are under 16, please do not use the app or provide any personal information. If we discover that a user under 16 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

a. Information You Provide

  • Onboarding Data: During onboarding, we collect your age, sex, stage of life (e.g., parent, student), spiritual maturity, and spiritual goals to personalize your experience. You may also provide an email address for account recovery or feedback purposes.
  • Chat Inputs: Text or voice inputs (transcribed via Google Speech-to-Text) you provide during conversations, prayer requests, or Bible study reflections.
  • Feedback: Written feedback submitted via the in-app feedback box or flagged responses, which may include your email for follow-up.

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • Chat History: The last 3 chat sessions and last 3 responses within the current session are stored to maintain conversation context, encrypted with AES-256.
  • Analytics Data: We collect anonymized data, including daily user counts, session duration, country, chat frequency, avatar usage, response length, and feature usage (e.g., prayer, Bible study).
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and IP address (anonymized) to ensure app functionality and security.

c. Third-Party Data

  • Google Speech-to-Text: Used for transcribing voice inputs. Google processes voice data under a Data Processing Agreement compliant with GDPR and CCPA.
  • Content APIs: We use open-source and public domain sources for Bible APIs (e.g., CPDV), Liturgical Calendar APIs (e.g., catholic-mass-readings, Church Calendar API), and Saints’ works. These APIs are called in-app and do not receive or process user personal data; they provide content only.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your experience with tailored responses, prayers, and Bible reflections based on your profile and chat history.
  • Maintain conversation context using summarized chat history (100-150 words).
  • Improve the app through anonymized analytics.
  • Respond to feedback or flagged responses via the admin dashboard.
  • Ensure app functionality and security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as follows:

  • Google Speech-to-Text: Processes voice inputs under a Data Processing Agreement compliant with GDPR and CCPA. No other personal data is shared with Google.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or legal process.
  • Admin Review: Flagged responses and feedback include your email for follow-up, accessible only to authorized admins via a secure dashboard.
  • Note: Content APIs (e.g., CPDV, catholic-mass-readings, Saints’ works) do not receive user personal data; they are used solely to retrieve open-source or public domain content within the app.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All personal data, including chat history, feedback, and analytics, is encrypted using AES-256 in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest.
  • Retention: Chat history and personal data are automatically deleted after 30 days unless you opt to delete earlier via settings. Anonymized analytics data is retained for 90 days for performance analysis.
  • Security Measures: We use secure authentication (OAuth 2.0) for admin dashboard access, conduct regular security audits, and maintain data breach notification protocols (e.g., 72-hour reporting under GDPR).

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete: Delete your data via settings or by contacting us.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Object: Opt out of analytics tracking.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rysen.app or use the “Data Management” section in the app settings.

7. Data Deletion

  • Chat history and personal data are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • You can manually delete your data at any time via the app’s settings.
  • Upon deletion, all associated data is permanently removed, except for anonymized analytics data, which may be retained for 90 days.

8. International Data Transfers

As a non-EU entity serving a global audience, we use Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure lawful cross-border data transfers for users in the European Union and other regions, in compliance with GDPR. For GDPR purposes, we will designate an EU representative if required, contactable at privacy@rysen.app.

9. Accessibility

RYSEN complies with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, offering high-contrast colors, screen reader support, and voice input compatibility to ensure accessibility for users with disabilities.

10. Third-Party Links

The app may include links to external sites (e.g., content API sources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies.

11. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@rysen.app
  • In-App: Use the feedback form in the app settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you via the app or email of significant changes. The updated policy will be effective upon posting in the app.
